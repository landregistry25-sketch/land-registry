Admin Guide: Managing Land Inspectors & Contact Messages

How to Create a Land Inspector

  1. Log in to the Admin Dashboard with your admin credentials.
  2. Navigate to the Land Inspectors management section.
  3. Click on the Add Inspector button.
  4. Fill out the inspector's details including name, email, username, and password.
  5. Assign appropriate permissions and roles to the new inspector.
  6. Submit the form to create the new land inspector account.
  7. Verify the creation by checking the land inspectors list for the new entry.

How to Delete a Land Inspector

  1. From the Land Inspectors management list, locate the inspector you want to delete.
  2. Click the Delete button or icon next to the inspector's name.
  3. Confirm the deletion action in the prompt to permanently remove the inspector.
  4. Ensure that deleting an inspector does not impact any ongoing verifications.
  5. Regularly review the inspector list to maintain updated records.

How to Respond to Contact Messages
