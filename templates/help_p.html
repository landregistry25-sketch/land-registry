Property Management Guide

How to View Your Property

  1. Login to your account on the platform.
  2. Navigate to the My Properties section from the main menu.
  3. You will see a list of properties you own, including key info such as location and size.
  4. Click on any property entry to open detailed view, including documents and status.
  5. Review your property documents, check verification status and request updates if needed.
Tip: Make sure your profile is complete for seamless property access.

How to Add a New Property

  1. Login to your account and go to Add Property from the dashboard or menu.
  2. Fill in the property details like owner name, address, size, and price in the form provided.
  3. Upload necessary documents and images such as ownership proof, maps, etc.
  4. Double-check all information for accuracy before submitting.
  5. Submit the form to register your property on the platform.
  6. Wait for verification and approval. You will be notified via email or notifications.
Note: Upload clear images and valid documents to speed up verification.

For assistance, contact our support team.
